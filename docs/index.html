GAME RULES

    - The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
    - In each turn, a player rolls 2 dices as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
    - BUT, if the player rolls one dice as a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
    - BUT, if the player rolls both dices as 6:6, all his GLOBAL score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
    - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
    - The first player to reach 100 points by default or points set by input field on GLOBAL score wins the game
